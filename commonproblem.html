Common Problem

        
1. Why can not connect to VPN?

  Please connect several times, switch to try other regions. If you still can not connect, please turn off the WIFI or switch traffic.
 
 
2. VIP account can bind how many devices?

  There is no theoretical limit, you can use your ID account to resume the purchase on other devices.
 
 
3. After deleting the app, vip information will disappear?

  After deleting the app, the vip information does not disappear. If you are replacing your device, use your ID recovery purchase to sync to any device.
